Rocksmith 2014: A quick introduction

What is Rocksmith?

Rocksmith is a videogame designed and published by Ubisoft. It is a rhythm videogame akin to RockBand and Guitar Hero with one big difference. In Rocksmith, to play the game you have to plug in an actual Electric Guitar! This fact in itself means that in addition to being a rhythm game, it is also considered a learning tool, helping millions to pick up the guitar and simply begin to play a myriad of songs.

Now one might wonder, what songs are available in Rocksmith 2014? Well let me tell you, there are currently over 450 songs that anyone can learn, with enough given practice. The songs are split into separate guitar tunings.

    Tunings
  • E Standard
  • Drop D
  • Eb Standard
  • Eb Drop Db
  • Drop C
  • D Standard
